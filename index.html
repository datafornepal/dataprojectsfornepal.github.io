Surveys and Assessments

Introduction to the Web & Spreadsheet Basics

The unit covers topics on the different components of the Web, importance of using spreadsheets, creating and working with spreadsheets, and performing basic calculations in spreadsheets.

Intro to Data Fundamentals and Finding Data Online

The unit covers topics on data-driven decision making, types of data, data visualizations, different formats of data, finding data online and navigating national databases.
